# 📊 Superstore Sales Data Analysis

## 📌 Project Overview

This project analyzes the Superstore retail dataset using Python to uncover business insights related to sales, profit, customer segments, product performance, regional trends, discounts, and shipping methods.

The goal is to demonstrate the complete data analysis process, from data cleaning and exploration to visualization and business recommendations.

---

## 🎯 Objectives

- Clean and prepare the dataset for analysis.
- Explore sales and profit performance.
- Identify the best-performing products and categories.
- Analyze regional sales and profitability.
- Examine the impact of discounts on profit.
- Discover seasonal sales trends.
- Provide actionable business recommendations.

---

## 🛠️ Technologies Used

- Python
- Pandas
- Matplotlib
- Seaborn
- Jupyter Notebook

---

## 📂 Dataset

The dataset contains **9,994 retail transactions** with **21 features**, including:

- Order Date
- Ship Date
- Category
- Sub-Category
- Product Name
- Sales
- Profit
- Discount
- Quantity
- Customer Segment
- Region
- Ship Mode

---

## 🧹 Data Preparation

The following preprocessing steps were completed:

- Checked for missing values
- Checked for duplicate records
- Converted `Order Date` and `Ship Date` to datetime format
- Explored data types and descriptive statistics

---

## 📈 Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)

The project answers the following business questions:

1. Which product category generates the highest sales?
2. Which region is the most profitable?
3. How do discounts affect profit?
4. How do sales change over time?
5. Which products generate the highest sales?
6. Which sub-categories are losing money?
7. Which shipping mode is used most frequently and which is the most profitable?

---

## 📊 Visualizations

The notebook includes:

- 📊 Bar Charts
- 📈 Line Charts
- 🔵 Scatter Plots

These visualizations help identify trends and support business decision-making.

---

## 💡 Key Insights

- Technology is the highest revenue-generating category.
- Profitability differs across regions.
- Higher discounts are generally associated with lower profits.
- Sales vary throughout the year, indicating seasonal trends.
- A small number of products contribute a large share of total sales.
- Some sub-categories generate losses despite generating sales.
- Shipping methods differ in usage and profitability.

---

## 📌 Business Recommendations

- Optimize discount strategies to improve profit margins.
- Increase investment in high-performing products and categories.
- Investigate and improve low-performing regions.
- Review loss-making sub-categories for pricing or product strategy.
- Plan inventory based on seasonal demand.
- Improve shipping efficiency while maintaining customer satisfaction.
